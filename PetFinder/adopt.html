Things to know before adoption

Welcoming a pet into your lives is a beautiful and exciting moment! It is also a time where you learn to provide a warm and comfortable environment to your pet. Here are a few things we thought are important to consider before adopting a pet:

  • Ensure that you have enough space for a pet in your house - especially after your pet grows up!
  • Are you someone who is away from home for most hours of the day? You must think of ways to manage your pet's feeding and exercise routine.
  • Spend time to get to know your pet before bringing them home - this helps them to establish trust in you.
  • If you already live with a pet, introduce your new furry friend to your pet and check if they can get along.
  • Pet proof your house and buy essential supplies like food, a collar and leash, a bed, grooming kit and medicines for your pet.
