Forum Rules

Forum Rules and Guidelines

Welcome to Bark: Boston University's first unofficial anonymous pet forum! Here are a few rules:

  • Be respectful of other users! I.e. Do not make rude remarks, call names, or post hateful messages. This is a shared space, so please be respectful to others!
  • Do not give out personal information!
  • Do not post anything illegal!
  • Respect the privacy of others. Do not harass other users for personal information!
  • Always be kind to others!

Guidelines

  1. Type out your post
  2. Hit the "Post" button
  3. If you see a green alert, then you Successfully posted!
  4. Your post is now officially part of the Bark forum! Congrats!

Happy Posting!
